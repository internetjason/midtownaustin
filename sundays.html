Sunday Morning Gatherings

Sunday Gatherings are a unique opportunity to gather around God to hear from his Word, consider his work, sing his praise, and enjoy his presence. They are also a place where those who are seeking, skeptical, or curious can come and explore the Christian faith with us.

Where & When

The Baker School

3908 Avenue B
Austin, TX 78751

Doors open at 10:30am for snacks, delicious Anderson's Coffee, and/or time to get your kiddos checked in for their class. Worship begins at 10:45am.

What to Expect

When you get there, grab some coffee and meet someone or just head right into the auditorium. Knowing that Jesus has truly paid it all for us helps our worship environment have a relaxed and casual feel. We will sing some songs, hear a sermon about Jesus from the Bible, take communion, and say a few prayers. Thatâ€™s about it! We welcome all people to join with us in our Sunday celebration of Jesus.

What About Kids?

While you are in the worship service we have fun and engaging classes for kids (nursery through elementary school) that will help them worship God according to their age level. We will always let you know what is being taught and classes are always optional.

We invite all middle school and high school students to worship with the adults.
